FAQ

Who works on Rocket?

Rocket has a core team of designers and developers dedicated to updating and maintaining the system. They are part of the Coles Group Human Centred Design (HCD) team.

How can I contribute ideas or feedback?

Join the Slack community and post your questions or comments in the relevant channels.

Which browsers are supported?
  • Chrome (Latest)
  • Firefox (Latest)
  • Safari (Latest)
  • Edge (Latest)
  • IE 11
What CSS naming convention does Rocket use?

Rocket follows the BEM (Block-Element-Modifier) convention.

What language are the components written in?

There are two implementations of Rocket : vanilla HTML/JS and React. The CSS is written in SASS.

Is Rocket accessible?

Rocket's components have all been tested against the WCAG 2.1 guidelines.
