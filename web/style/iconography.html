Iconography

Rocket icons are elegantly crafted symbols for common actions and items. They communicate a message and should be distinct and informative, and be used sparingly throughout the product to provide clarity and reduce cognitive load on users.

Includes SVG, PNG, icon font, Sketch file, and Nucleo library.
